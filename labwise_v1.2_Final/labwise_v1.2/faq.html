General Questions

Labwise Medical Centre is open 24/7, providing round-the-clock healthcare services. Our emergency services are available at all times, while scheduled appointments can be made during regular business hours (8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Monday to Sunday).

We are located at 123 Medical Plaza, Suite 100, Demo City, DC 12345, Zimbabwe. Our facility is easily accessible with ample parking available for patients and visitors.

Yes, we accept most major medical insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card and valid ID to your appointment. We also offer cash payment options and payment plans for uninsured patients.

Appointments & Booking

You can book an appointment through our online booking system, by calling us at +1 (555) 123-4567, or by visiting our facility in person. Online booking is available 24/7 for your convenience.

Yes, we offer same-day appointments based on availability. For urgent medical needs, we recommend calling ahead or visiting our emergency services. Walk-in patients are also welcome, though appointment holders receive priority.

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card (if applicable), a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results. Arrive 15 minutes early for check-in procedures.

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment by calling us at +1 (555) 123-4567 at least 24 hours in advance. This helps us accommodate other patients who may need urgent care.

Services & Procedures

We offer comprehensive healthcare services including general practice consultations, pediatrics, dermatology, orthopedics, laboratory testing, emergency care, and diagnostic services. Our experienced medical team provides personalized care for all ages.

Yes, we have a fully equipped diagnostic laboratory offering blood tests, pathology services, rapid diagnostic testing, and other laboratory procedures. Most results are available within 24-48 hours.

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency medical services. Our emergency department is staffed with qualified medical professionals ready to handle urgent medical situations and critical care needs.

Payment & Insurance

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, mobile money transfers, and medical insurance. Payment plans are available for certain procedures. Please discuss payment options with our billing department.

Consultation fees vary depending on the type of service and specialist involved. Please contact us at +1 (555) 123-4567 for current pricing information. We strive to keep our services affordable and accessible.

COVID-19 & Safety

We follow strict hygiene protocols including regular sanitization, mandatory mask wearing, temperature checks, social distancing measures, and enhanced cleaning procedures to ensure the safety of all patients and staff.

Yes, we offer COVID-19 testing including rapid antigen tests and PCR tests. Please call ahead to schedule your test and discuss which type is most appropriate for your needs.
